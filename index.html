Pug Fun Facts!

♦They usually weigh about 14-18 lbs
♦They are about 10-14 inches tall at the shoulder
♦They live for about 12-15 years

More Facts!

♦They tend to shed a lot
♦They wheeze and snore loudly
♦They are high energy and require lots of companionship
